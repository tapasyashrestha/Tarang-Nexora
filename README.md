# Tarang – Prototype Overview

RippleIQ is an AI-powered decision intelligence platform that helps e-commerce businesses understand the ripple effects of strategic decisions before implementation.

The prototype allows users to modify business variables such as product price, inventory levels, and marketing spend, then simulates their impact on key metrics including sales, revenue, delivery performance, and customer satisfaction.

Using machine learning, dependency mapping, and scenario simulation, RippleIQ identifies hidden relationships between business variables and detects potential risks such as inventory shortages, delivery delays, and rating declines.

### Prototype Features

* Interactive Business Dashboard
* Dependency & Causality Mapping
* What-If Scenario Simulator
* Revenue & Sales Forecasting
* Risk & Consequence Detection
* Strategic Recommendations

### Tech Stack

* Frontend: React, Tailwind CSS, Recharts
* Backend: FastAPI
* ML: Scikit-Learn, XGBoost, Pandas
* Database: PostgreSQL

### Goal

Enable businesses to visualize cascading impacts, forecast future outcomes, and make smarter data-driven decisions with confidence.
